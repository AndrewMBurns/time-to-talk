time to talk.

Time to Talk is a campaign to get Ireland's parents talking about online safety.

30% of children in Ireland are never talking to their parents about online safety. With Time to Talk, we believe we can change that.

We have three clear steps to better communication.

Look.
Learn.
Talk.
These three steps will make this process a little easier. Time to Talk is for all parents. It doesn't matter if your child is very young or if you think you've left it too late.

The first step to keeping our children safe online is looking to see when they need our help.

Look.
If we know where to look and what to look out for, it will be a lot easier to keep our children safe online. It's about keeping an eye on them, and recognising when they need our help.

It's hard to help if we feel uneducated. We can teach you the basics.

Learn.
It's easy to feel overwhelmed! As if we've left it too late to get our heads around technology and how it works. There's no need to worry. We'll give you the basics.

Once that's done, it's smooth sailing. We just have to talk about it.

Talk.
This is the easy part! It's important that we keep the conversation going. Let's talk to our children and share what we learn with other parents.

Simple, right? Let's get started with look.
