The idea of talking to our children can be terrifying, but it doesn't have to be! The main thing is to talk regularly about online issues, in the same way we would with offline issues.

How often should we be talking to our children?

Everyday.
As often as we can is the short answer. But, everyone is different. Talking about online safety should be as normal as talking about their day at school. It just has to be frequent.

Sounds good. What should we say?

Conversation starters.

That looks
fun! How do you play?

Would you mind having a chat?

Do you want
to ask me anything?

Educate eachother.

Do you know how to block someone?

Where do you go for help online?

What's the
best part of Snapchat?

It doesn't matter what we say, the main thing is that we say something. Try simple questions, that aren't going to frighten anyone (including you). Just start that conversation.

Okay, and when should we bring it up?

At the dinner table.
On the way to school.
These are just a few ideas to get you started. The main thing is to bring up the topic of online safety everywhere and anywhere, just like we would with anything else!
